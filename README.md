## ğŸ”‘ Key Insights

### 1. **Accelerated Growth with Supply Chain Vulnerabilities**
Revenue grew **300% from 2020-2022**, reaching **$24.9M**, but anomaly detection revealed a critical **$195K revenue drop in December 2021**, highlighting supply chain vulnerabilities that require operational review.

### 2. **Product Portfolio Balance & Cross-Selling Opportunities**
Bikes drive **55% of orders** while maintaining a healthy **2.2% return rate**. Accessories show strong cross-selling potential with **Water Bottles leading at 3,983 units sold** ($39.7K revenue), suggesting bundling opportunities.

### 3. **Customer Retention Optimization Potential**
Customer analysis shows an average of **1.45 orders per customer** with **17.4K unique customers**. Top 100 customers generated substantial revenue, indicating significant upsell potential and the need for targeted loyalty programs.
